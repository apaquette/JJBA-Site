Summary

JoJoâ€™s Bizarre Adventure is the story of the Joestar family. Each arc follows the life of another member of the Joestar family, tracing their adventures through the generations. The first arc, Phantom Blood, follows Jonathan Joestar in England during the 1880s. The second arc, Battle Tendency, follows Joseph Joestar in America in the late 1930s. The third arc, Stardust Crusaders, follows Jotaro Kujo as he travels from Japan to Egypt in the late 1980s. The Fourth arc, Diamond is Unbreakable, follows Josuke Higashikata in Japan in 1999. The fifth arc, Golden Wind, follows Giorno Giovanna in Italy in 2001.
